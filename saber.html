Saber our friendly cat

Saber is a brown tabby. She is over 10 years old now. Her eyes are almost always dilated because she is going blind. This hasn't really slowed her down any, and vets have told us that cats do not rely on sight too much.

Saber's personality and look

Saber has a very fun fur pattern. I like how her tail is all black at the end so it's almost like it's tipped in ink. Saber likes having a collar on. Once when we took it off, we noticed that she was a little depressed and would constantly lay near the collar and try to roll on it. After we put the collar back on she was back to her usual cheerful self.

Some things she likes

Saber loves to eat wet food and something that always get's her attention is the sound of a can of cat food being opened, or tapped. It's fun because if she's in a higher level of the house and we open the can we can hear her scurrying off her cat tower down to the kitchen.

Saber is a very easy going cat and over the years we've been able to get her to wear a lot of costumes and hats.
