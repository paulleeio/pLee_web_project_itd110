  1. Are they indoor or outside cats?

    Indoor cats. We've had them outside before, but they both prefer indoors. Cassini hates outdoors and will cling to the wall when outside. I think this is due to her growing up as a feral cat.

  2. Who is older?

    Saber is older. Although Cassini is more responsible. It's hard to tell which cat is older because of them both being rescues. Many of these organizations don't have strong bookkeeping.

  3. Who is smarter?

    Cassini is smarter. She was able to solve puzzles much faster than Saber

  4. Who is more friendly?

    Saber is way more friendly. She will plop down on the lap of anyone. Cat sitters have sent pictures of Saber cuddling with them before.

  5. Do they play together?

    Rarely. They like being in the same room and area as each other but not playing together. It's very odd. The closest they've been is when we moved. They were unsure of the environment and would share a cat carrier voluntarily

  6. Who do the cat's like better?

    Saber hands down likes my wife more than she likes me. Cassini usually likes me better but it can vary. Cat emotions are very fickle.

  7. Who do you like more?

    Like any good fur parent, I love both cats equally.
