Cassini's Habits

Cassini has a resting grumpy face but is a very sweet cat. She has a habit of liking enclosed spaces like boxes, and cubbies. I think this is related to her time as a feral cat. Cassini has very soft fur and does a good job of keeping herself well groomed.

She likes her caves

In the picture below, I was able to find a cat bed cubby that fits in a cube shelf slot. This ended up being a great purchase and Cassini spends a lot of time in there. Cassini has a lot of fur so there's always a bunch of fur in the cube!

Judgemental cat

Cassini's main habits are to look out the window and judge people. Her resting grumpy face helps with this. It makes it seem like she's judging everyone harshly. There was a time when my wife and I were living in an 11th floor apartment. This was the golden age of Cassini's window watching days. We would try to look for her whenever we left the house.

Cool cat gallery

Cassini is also a great hunter. Whenever there is a bug flying around he house and Cassini spots it, she goes into attack mode. She will make this chittering sound and then track the bug. I've seen her catch a lot of flies, June bugs, and the like. Cassini also will make chittering sounds when she sees a bird get close by.

Cassini passing judgement. Cassini also is great at grooming herself. Most people note how soft she is. She's a very soft cat!
