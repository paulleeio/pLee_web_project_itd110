Our Cool Cats

Welcome to the website about the cats at Lee's house. This is a site dedicated to the two great cats at my house. Their names are Saber and Cassini.

How we met

Both cats were originally my wife's before we got married. Once we tied the knot on March 25, 2017, the cats came along for the ride. The cats have added a lot of fun to my life so I wanted to create a web page to honor them. Some more information about the cats:

Helpful guide

  • Saber

    My wife adopted Saber (brown tabby) first. She named Saber after her favorite character from the Anime Fate Stay Night. Saber (the cat) does not really live up to the name of Saber from the anime. Our Saber is a very lazy cat and does not have a lot of responsibilities. We still love her all the same as she's very friendly and loving. Saber loves sitting in people's laps and will ingratiate herself to anyone. Guests will often find Saber trying to sneak her way into their laps.

  • Cassini

    My wife adopted Cassini (tuxedo) second. Cassini was named after one of the satellites orbiting Saturn. This was because my wife's favorite sailor in Sailor Moon is Sailor Saturn. Cassini was originally a feral cat but was found by a cat rescue. Cassini was very skittish at first but she eventually warmed up to us. Now she loves lazing luxuriously and getting brushed. This doesn't extend to new people though. She usually does not warm up to a guest until they've been to our house a few times.
